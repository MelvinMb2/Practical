Welcome To MB2

Mutiara Bangsa 2 School is a school under the auspices of the Ananda Bina Smart Foundation. Mutiara Bangsa began to be inscribed with gold ink for the first time in 2003 under the name Mutiara Bangsa 1 which is located in the Poris Indah housing complex, Tangerang. Under the auspices of the Mutiara Bangsa Foundation, this school uses the national curriculum as the basis for its teaching and learning activities. Due to the amount of support and trust from parents of students given to Mutiara Bangsa, in 2005 Mutiara Bangsa expanded its wings by building Mutiara Bangsa 3 Kindergarten, Elementary, Middle School, High School in Jelambar Barat III, West Jakarta. It did not stop there, Mutiara Bangsa School continued its work in the world of education by building Mutiara Bangsa 2 in 2008 which is located at Jalan Husein Sastranegara no 29 B, Tangerang. Located in front of the Duta Garden residential complex, Tangerang.

MB2

Mutiara Bangsa 2 was built in 2008 which is located at Jalan Husein Sastranegara no 29 B, Tangerang. Located in front of the Duta Garden residential complex, Tangerang.

Each class in Mutiara Bangsa 2 has less than 30 students. This number of students already meets good national standards and helps to provide a balanced interaction between students and create a close relationship between students and teachers. Mutiara Bangsa 2 School is also actively participating in various competitions at the city, provincial and national levels.

Education System
